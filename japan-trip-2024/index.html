Japan Trip 2024

A trip to Japan in 2024 visited Tokyo, Kyoto, and Atami and had a great time at all three locations.

Shinjuku Golden Gai

Golden Gai is a small area of 200+ tiny bars crammed into a network of 6 alleyways. You are guaranteed an intersting night-out here.

DisneySea

We were on the fence about going to a Disney park in Japan, but DisneySea turned out to be one of our favorites. The street food stands throughout the park were amazing. The attractions and rides were unique and just as fun as US parks.

TeamLab Museum

The TeamLab "Borderless" Digital Art Museum was great - probably the best digital art museum we've seen anywhere (and we've been to quite a few).

Kyoto

Kyoto is the place that I dream of returning to, maybe every week. It's everything - the food, culture, architecture, streets, people, etc. create a tranquil vibe that just has to be experienced.

Walk through Arashiyama

An unassuming walk through Arashiyama to Otaga Nenbutsugi Temple became one of my lasting impressions of Japan - something about the foliage, decore of the neighborhood houses, and the way they are arranged. We started the walk at the bamboo forest and then walked through the neighborhood streets to the temple. There was a pottery shop along the way where we picked up some coffee mugs and saké sets - we had some serious regrets later about not picking out more things there to bring home.

Atami Airbnb w/ private onsen

Finding an Airbnb with a private onsen was as great as it sounds. This place was in a really beautiful spot. Could have spent a week just enjoying the onsen, but we took a nice walk to town through a park with a waterfall to hit some cafes, stores, and restaurants. One thing to be aware of is that the onsen isn't guaranteed to be running, as it depends on the water flow from the mountain. I saw maybe 1 or 2 reviews complaining that it wasn't working on their stay.
